Drent

Using this platform the current problem of maintaining rental records and implementing agreements will be solved to a very great extent.

In this platform the property owners can upload their property details and the tenant can see available properties directly fetching data from blockchain. This removes the need of any middleman or broker hence, reducing the cost of the procedure.

This adds transparency as the owner and tenant are directly connected. All the rental agreements are maintained on smart contracts and can not be corrupted by anyone.
